Sxript Alpha Portal
Introduction
Sxript Alpha, shamelessly named after Wolfram Alpha, is an instance of qbot64 that receives and replies to commands (in Sxript or otherwise) in a REPL-like fashion. The connection may be via Internet or Lan TCP/IP, direct through open ports, and of course, by IRC (channel #sxript on irc.freenode.net).

Instructions
  • For same-computer connections, use address 127.0.0.1:10314.

  • For same-network connections, use the local address of the host, such as 10.0.0.215:10314.

  • For Internet connections, use the wide-area address of the host machine. For example, 174.63.15.43:10314.
