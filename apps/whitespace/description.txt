The Sxript interpreter ignores <i>all</i> whitespace that is not part of a quote. Whitespace characters outside of quotes are generated by <font face="Courier New">\ </font> (slash-space), <font face="Courier New">\t</font> (slash-[t]ab), and <font face="Courier New">\n</font> (slash-[n]ew line). The backslash is the general-use escape character.
